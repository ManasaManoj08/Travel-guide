Summer sands beach resort

Nestled along the pristine shores of Mangalore, Summer Sand Beach Resort stands as a serene escape where sun, sand, and luxury converge. This coastal haven welcomes guests to a world of tranquility, offering a perfect blend of relaxation and recreation.
The resort's architecture reflects a harmonious marriage of modern design and coastal aesthetics. The spacious rooms and suites, adorned with contemporary furnishings, provide breathtaking views of the azure Arabian Sea or the lush greenery surrounding the property.
Step outside, and the private beach beckons—a secluded stretch of golden sand where guests can unwind, indulge in water sports, or simply bask in the warmth of the sun. The resort's expansive swimming pool, fringed with swaying palm trees, offers a refreshing retreat for those seeking a break from the salty waves.
