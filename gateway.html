The Gateway Hotel Old Port Road

The Gateway Hotel Old Port Road stands as an epitome of luxury and comfort in the heart of Mangalore. This distinguished hotel, part of the Taj Group, welcomes guests with a blend of contemporary elegance and traditional charm. Located on the iconic Old Port Road, it offers a strategic vantage point to explore the city's rich culture and business hubs.
The hotel's architecture exudes a timeless appeal, reflecting Mangalore's cultural heritage. Step into a world of refined hospitality as the courteous staff ensures a seamless stay. The rooms and suites are tastefully appointed, providing a perfect blend of comfort and sophistication.
Culinary experiences at The Gateway Hotel are a treat for the senses. Indulge in a diverse array of cuisines at the on-site restaurants, each offering a distinctive ambiance and a menu curated with culinary expertise.
Whether you're a business traveler making use of the well-equipped conference facilities or a leisure seeker enjoying the pool and spa, The Gateway Hotel Old Port Road offers a holistic experience. The lush surroundings and warm hospitality make it a haven for those seeking a luxurious retreat in the vibrant city of Mangalore.
