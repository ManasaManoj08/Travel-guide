City Centre Mall

City Center Mall is a prominent shopping destination located in the heart of Mangalore, Karnataka, India. Spread across multiple floors, this modern shopping complex offers a diverse range of retail outlets, entertainment options, and dining experiences, making it a popular hub for locals and visitors alike.
The mall features a well-curated selection of national and international brands, catering to various fashion, lifestyle, and electronic needs. Shoppers can explore a wide array of clothing, accessories, electronics, and home goods within the contemporary and inviting environment of City Center Mall.
Apart from shopping, the mall provides a range of entertainment choices.
