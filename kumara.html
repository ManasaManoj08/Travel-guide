Kumara parvatha(Pushpagiri)

Embark on a thrilling adventure with the Kumara Parvatha Trek, an iconic trail nestled in the Western Ghats near Mangalore. Also known as Pushpagiri, Kumara Parvatha stands as the second-highest peak in Karnataka, offering intrepid trekkers an exhilarating journey through lush forests, rolling hills, and breathtaking landscapes.
The trek commences at the quaint village of Kukke Subramanya, weaving its way through dense, evergreen forests and ascending through challenging terrains. As you ascend, the trail unfolds panoramic vistas of the surrounding hills, valleys, and vibrant flora, creating a picturesque backdrop.
The trek is a test of both physical endurance and mental fortitude, with steep inclines and rocky paths leading to the summit. Upon reaching the peak, trekkers are rewarded with awe-inspiring views, especially during the early morning sunrise or late evening sunset, when the landscape is bathed in hues of gold and crimson.
Camping enthusiasts can choose to spend a night amidst nature, adding a touch of wilderness to their adventure. The descent offers a chance to soak in the tranquility of the Western Ghats, with streams and waterfalls providing refreshing pitstops along the way.
Kumara Parvatha Trek isn't just a physical challenge; it's an immersive experience into the unspoiled beauty of Karnataka's mountainous terrain, making it a must-visit for nature enthusiasts and avid trekkers looking to conquer the heights of the Western Ghats.
